World History Insider

About

World History Insider is our exclusive members' magazine. Every month our members receive this digital magazine, filled with over 40 pages containing:

  • Editorials
  • Behind the Scenes
  • Team News
  • Content Highlights
  • Map Highlights
  • Interviews
  • Exhibitions
  • Archaeology News
  • Object of the Month
  • Book Reviews

The magazine is exclusively available to our subscribing members and is delivered by email every month. If you're interested, you can read a sample issue for free. To subscribe, please sign up to become a member and support our non-profit organization!
