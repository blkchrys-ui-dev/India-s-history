Needed Content



While we always wish to expand our coverage of all subjects in history, this page lists those cultures and topics where we are especially looking for submissions. If you are knowledgeable about any of the subject areas below, please do approach us to submit content, or if you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to email us at (editor AT worldhistory.org). Our editors will be happy to advise you on the style, content and formats our articles should include.


We are open to submissions on any subject, but we are especially looking for submissions on the following subject areas:

  • The Spanish Empire
  • The Holy Roman Empire
  • Islam
  • Judaism
  • Scythians
  • Berbers
  • Muslim Caliphates
  • Stone Age Cultures
  • Gupta Empire
  • Stone Age Cultures
  • Medieval China
